# Animated PM Notification

## Install

1. Download the latest release and unzip the downloaded release.
2. Copy `sniper/anipmnotify/*.*` folder to your forum `root/ext/sniper/anipmnotify/*.*`
5. Navigate in the ACP to `Customise -> Manage extensions`.
6. Look for `Animated PM Notification` under the Disabled Extensions list, and click its `Enable` link.

## Uninstall

1. Navigate in the ACP to `Customise -> Extension Management -> Extensions`.
2. Look for `Animated PM Notification` under the Enabled Extensions list, and click its `Disable` link.
3. To permanently uninstall, click `Delete Data` and then delete the `/ext/sniper/anipmnotify` folder.
